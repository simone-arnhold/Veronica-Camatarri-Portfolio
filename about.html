About Me

Since a young age, I have had a strong passion for art and have consistently taken part in various art competitions throughout my academic journey, starting from grade school all the way to college.

I possess a degree in Artistic Design for the Enterprise, which has broadened my horizons and helped me to discover various traditional and visual art forms, as well as new artists who have motivated me to be more innovative with my creative abilities.

After completing my education, I have gained hands-on experience working in a range of roles as a designer and producer for both public and private organizations.

My focus has been on experimenting with different art styles, with a specific emphasis on the areas of rapid communication, advertising, and digital illustration.
